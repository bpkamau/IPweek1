About us:

We are a restaurant located in Kilimani, situated the leafy suburbs of Nairobi. We pride ourselves as one of the best food joints in Nairobi due to our original Kenyan cuisine and quality service that we provide along with the good food. We are proud to say that we have enjoyed some success due to our unique cuisine.

We are famous for our delicious chapati and people come from far and wide just to have a taste of our unique recipes.

Our menu consists of:

  • White chapati.
  • Brown chapati.
  • Carrot chapati.
